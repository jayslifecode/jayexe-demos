What we offer

BESPOKE BLINDS & SHADING SYSTEMS

Every product is made to your exact specifications and installed by our Bristol-based team. From domestic roller blinds to large-scale commercial shading — we cover it all.

01

Roller Blinds

The most versatile blind on the market. Available in blackout, dim-out, screen and light-filtering fabrics — custom-made to fit any window in your Bristol home or office.

02

Venetian Blinds

Timeless aluminium or real wood venetians offering precise slat-by-slat light control. Perfect for kitchens, offices and period properties throughout Bristol.

03

Pleated & Duette Blinds

Energy-efficient honeycomb construction that traps air for insulation. Ideal for conservatories and roof windows where temperature management matters most.

04

Vertical Blinds

Ideal for wide windows, sliding doors and patio glazing. Available in a vast range of fabrics and colours — made to measure for a precise, clean finish.

05

Roman Blinds

Soft, elegant fabric folds that add warmth to any room. Custom-made in hundreds of fabrics — from sheer linens to blackout linings — for a perfectly co-ordinated look.

06

Commercial Shading Systems

Motorised roller blinds, solar shading and blackout systems for offices, schools, healthcare and hospitality venues across Bristol and the South West.
