Our Products & Services

Window Treatments Built for the South

From classic plantation shutters to motorized shades, every product is custom-made to complement Birmingham's finest homes.

Plantation Shutters

The gold standard of window treatments. Our solid-wood plantation shutters offer timeless curb appeal, superior light control, and lasting value for your home.

Custom Shades

Roman, roller, cellular, and woven wood shades crafted to your exact specifications — bringing softness and elegance to every room.

Wood Blinds

Real hardwood blinds that warm any interior with natural beauty. Available in dozens of stains and finishes to complement your decor perfectly.

Faux Wood Blinds

The look of real wood with superior moisture resistance — ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-humidity spaces throughout your Alabama home.

Motorized Window Treatments

Control your shades and blinds with a touch — or hands-free via smart home integration. The ultimate convenience for large windows and vaulted spaces.
