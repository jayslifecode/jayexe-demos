About Us

BIRMINGHAM'S TRUSTED WINDOW TREATMENT STUDIO

Louver Shop of Birmingham is a locally-owned window treatment studio dedicated to transforming homes with premium, custom-crafted shutters, blinds, and shades.

We believe the perfect window treatment is one that fits your home exactly — which is why every product is custom-measured, custom-built, and installed by our own certified team.

Located at 137 W Oxmoor Rd, we serve homeowners and builders across Birmingham, Hoover, Vestavia Hills, Mountain Brook, and all of Jefferson County.
