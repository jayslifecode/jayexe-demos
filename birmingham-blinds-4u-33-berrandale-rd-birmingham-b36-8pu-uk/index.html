What we supply & fit

MADE-TO-MEASURE BLINDS FOR EVERY WINDOW

From simple roller blinds to day & night dual fabric systems — we stock a wide range of styles and measure every window ourselves for a perfect fit.

01

Roller Blinds

Our most popular choice. Clean, simple, and available in blackout, light-filter, and printed fabrics. Made to your exact measurements for every room.

02

Venetian Blinds

Aluminium and real wood venetians for precise light and privacy control. A timeless choice for kitchens, bathrooms, and home offices across Birmingham.

03

Vertical Blinds

Ideal for large windows, patio doors, and conservatories. Available in fabric, PVC, and blackout finishes with a wide choice of colours and textures.

04

Roman Blinds

Elegant fabric roman blinds that fold neatly when raised. Perfect for adding warmth and style to living rooms, bedrooms, and bay windows.

05

Day & Night Blinds

Dual-fabric roller blinds with alternating sheer and blackout zones, letting you control light and privacy precisely throughout the day.

06

Supply & Fit Service

We handle everything — measuring, ordering, and fitting. Our own fitters ensure every blind is hung level, tight, and exactly as specified.
