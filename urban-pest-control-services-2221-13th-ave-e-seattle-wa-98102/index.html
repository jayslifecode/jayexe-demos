What We Eliminate

Comprehensive Pest Solutions

From rodents to stinging insects, we handle every pest that threatens your home or business with targeted, effective treatments.

Rodent Control

Complete elimination of mice and rats with bait stations, trapping, and exclusion work to seal entry points and prevent re-infestation.

Cockroach Elimination

Targeted gel baiting and residual treatments that destroy cockroach colonies at the source — including German and American varieties.

Ant Control

Identification and elimination of ant species common to Seattle including carpenter ants, odorous house ants, and pavement ants.

Bed Bug Treatment

Thorough inspection and multi-stage treatment protocol to fully eliminate bed bug infestations with follow-up verification.

Wasp & Hornet Removal

Safe removal of yellow jackets, paper wasps, and bald-faced hornets — nests located, treated, and removed to prevent return.

Spider Control

Reduction of spider populations including hobo spiders and house spiders through web removal and residual barrier treatments.
