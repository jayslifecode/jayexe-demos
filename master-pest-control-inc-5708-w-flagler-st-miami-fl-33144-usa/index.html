What We Treat

Every pest, every time.

Comprehensive treatments for Miami's most common household and commercial pests. We identify the problem, eliminate it at the source, and prevent it from coming back.

Ant Control

Fire ants, carpenter ants, ghost ants — we treat colonies at the source and seal entry points to keep them out for good.

Cockroach Elimination

German, American, and palmetto bugs don't stand a chance. Targeted gel baits and residual sprays clear infestations fast.

Wasp & Bee Removal

Safe removal of nests from eaves, attics, and outdoor structures. We protect your family without harming pollinators unnecessarily.

Termite Treatment

Subterranean and drywood termite treatments including liquid barriers and bait stations. Protect your biggest investment.

Rodent Control

Rats and mice eliminated with trapping, exclusion, and sanitation. We seal entry points and remove attractants.

Bed Bug Treatment

Heat treatment and chemical protocols that eliminate bed bugs at every life stage — eggs, nymphs, and adults.
