What We Do

COMPREHENSIVE PEST CONTROL SERVICES

From prevention to elimination — targeted treatments using proven methods, safe for your family and aligned with Massachusetts environmental standards.

01

General Pest Control

Comprehensive inspection and treatment for common household pests. Customized plans that address current infestations and guard against future ones.

02

Rodent Exclusion & Control

Full rodent management: baiting, trapping, and structural exclusion to seal entry points for good. We eliminate the problem and prevent recurrence.

03

Ant & Stinging Insect Control

Targeted treatments for carpenter ants, pavement ants, wasps, yellow jackets, and hornets — protecting your property and outdoor spaces safely.

04

Bed Bug Treatment

Thorough inspection and multi-phase treatment to eliminate bed bug infestations completely. Heat and chemical options available with full follow-up.

05

Wildlife Removal

Humane removal of nuisance wildlife including squirrels, raccoons, skunks, and bats. Removal paired with exclusion to prevent re-entry.

06

Seasonal Prevention Plans

Year-round protection tailored to New England's pest seasons. Quarterly visits keep your home and business pest-free through every season.
