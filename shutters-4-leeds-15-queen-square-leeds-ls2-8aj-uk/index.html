Our Shutters

Crafted for Every Window

From classic full-height panels to bespoke special shapes — we design, supply and fit interior shutters tailored precisely to your home.

Full Height Shutters

Our most popular style — floor-to-ceiling panels that provide maximum light control and a timeless look for any room.

Tier-on-Tier Shutters

Independent upper and lower panels give you the flexibility to open the top for light whilst keeping the bottom closed for privacy.

Café Style Shutters

Covering the lower half of your window only — perfect for street-facing rooms where you want natural light whilst retaining privacy.

Bay Window Shutters

Precisely engineered to follow the angles of bay windows, creating a seamless, bespoke finish that off-the-shelf products simply cannot match.

Tracked Shutters

Sliding on a track system — ideal for wide openings, bi-fold doors and patio doors where traditional hinged panels won't suit.

Special Shape Shutters

Arched, circular, triangular or any non-standard shape — our craftsmen create shutters that fit perfectly, however unusual the window.
