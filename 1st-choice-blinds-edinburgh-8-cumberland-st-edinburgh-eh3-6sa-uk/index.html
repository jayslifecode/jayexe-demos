Our Products

Window Treatments for Every Room

From elegant Roman blinds to contemporary roller shades, our full range is made-to-measure and professionally installed.

Roller Blinds

Sleek, streamlined and available in hundreds of fabrics — from sheer to blackout. The perfect modern solution for any room.

Roman Blinds

Classic elegance with soft fabric folds. Ideal for Edinburgh's Georgian townhouses and period properties seeking timeless style.

Venetian Blinds

Precision tilt control over light and privacy. Available in aluminium, wood and faux wood to suit every interior.

Vertical Blinds

Versatile louvres perfect for large windows, patio doors, and office spaces. Excellent light management at scale.

Blackout Blinds

Total darkness when you need it most. Ideal for bedrooms, nurseries, and home cinemas. Lined for energy efficiency too.

Plantation Shutters

Timeless craftsmanship that transforms any window into a design feature. Fully bespoke, expertly fitted for Edinburgh homes.
