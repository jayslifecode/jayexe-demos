What We Offer

OUR WINDOW
TREATMENT RANGE

From solar shades to motorized smart systems — we carry and install every style of window treatment for homes and businesses across Boston.

01

Roller & Solar Shades

Clean, modern roller shades in hundreds of fabrics — including solar sheer options that reduce glare while preserving your view of the city.

02

Wood & Faux Wood Blinds

Warm, classic horizontal blinds in real hardwood and moisture-resistant faux wood. Perfect for Boston's historic homes and modern condos alike.

03

Plantation Shutters

Custom plantation shutters built to your exact window dimensions. Timeless style that adds real value to any Boston home.

04

Motorized Smart Blinds

App-controlled and voice-activated window treatments. Compatible with Alexa, Google Home and Apple HomeKit for seamless smart-home integration.

05

Cellular Honeycomb Shades

Superior insulation for Boston winters and summers. Single, double and triple-cell options to maximize energy efficiency in your home.

06

Custom Drapery

Bespoke drapery panels crafted to your exact specifications. Hundreds of designer fabrics — lined, interlined and blackout options available.
