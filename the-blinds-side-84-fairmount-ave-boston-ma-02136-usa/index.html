Our Products

Every Window, Every Style

From sleek roller shades to rich wood blinds, we carry premium treatments for every room and budget — all custom-fitted to your exact windows.

Roller Shades

Clean, minimal, and endlessly customizable. Solar, light-filtering, or blackout — we'll find the perfect opacity for your space.

Venetian Blinds

Timeless horizontal slats that give you total control over light and privacy. Available in aluminum, faux wood, and real wood finishes.

Wood Blinds

Warm, natural, and undeniably classic. Our real wood blinds bring texture and warmth to any room with rich stain options to match your decor.

Cellular Shades

Honeycomb construction traps air for superior insulation — keeping your Boston home comfortable year-round while reducing energy bills.

Vertical Blinds

Ideal for sliding doors and large windows. Smooth operation, easy maintenance, and available in fabric, vinyl, and wood-look finishes.

Custom Drapery

Elevate any room with custom-sewn drapes tailored to your windows, style, and fabric choice. Luxury look, professional installation.
