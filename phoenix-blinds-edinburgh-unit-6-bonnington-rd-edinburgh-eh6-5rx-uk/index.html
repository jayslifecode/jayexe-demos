Our Story

PHOENIX BLINDS
EDINBURGH

Based in Bonnington Road, Phoenix Blinds Edinburgh has been supplying and fitting made-to-measure window blinds across the city. We work directly with Edinburgh homeowners, interior designers, and commercial clients to deliver bespoke window solutions.

Every order begins with a free home survey. Our surveyor visits your property, takes precise measurements, advises on the best solutions for your needs, and provides a no-obligation quote. All blinds are then manufactured and installed by our own expert team.
