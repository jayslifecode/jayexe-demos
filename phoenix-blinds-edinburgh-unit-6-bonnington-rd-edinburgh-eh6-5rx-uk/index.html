Our Range

Blinds & Shutters
Made for Edinburgh

Six specialist products, all made-to-measure and fitted by our Edinburgh-based team.

Roller Blinds

Smooth, clean lines with a vast range of fabrics — from sheer daylight to total blackout. Perfect for any room in your Edinburgh home.

Vertical Blinds

Ideal for large windows and patio doors. Adjustable louvres give precise light and privacy control throughout the day.

Venetian Blinds

Classic aluminium or real wood slats, tilted to your angle. Timeless style with outstanding light management.

Roman Blinds

Soft fabric folds that bring warmth and elegance. Made-to-measure with hundreds of premium textiles to choose from.

Blackout Blinds

Complete light block for bedrooms, nurseries and media rooms. Available in roller and roman styles with sealed side channels.

Plantation Shutters

Solid hardwood or MDF shutters for lasting kerb appeal. A premium upgrade that adds value to Edinburgh properties.
