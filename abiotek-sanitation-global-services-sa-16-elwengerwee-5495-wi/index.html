What We Do

Comprehensive Sanitation & Pest Management

From initial audit to preventive maintenance — targeted treatments using certified methods, fully compliant with European regulatory frameworks.

Insect Control

Targeted treatments for crawling and flying insects. Full identification, treatment plan, and follow-up monitoring for long-term elimination.

Rodent Management

Professional treatment for rats, mice, and other rodents. Integrated pest management combining baiting, exclusion, and environmental assessment.

Disinfection & Decontamination

Complete surface and air disinfection using CE-certified biocidal products — suitable for food production, healthcare, and hospitality environments.

Hygiene Audits

Independent HACCP-aligned audits with written reports. Identify risks, close compliance gaps, and meet BRC, IFS, or ISO 22000 standards.

Fumigation Services

Controlled fumigation for warehouses, food storage, and transport vehicles. Full documentation supplied for regulatory records and audit trails.

Preventive Programmes

Scheduled preventive treatments tailored to your facility risk profile — reducing the probability of infestation before it disrupts operations.
