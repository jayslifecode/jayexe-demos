What We Do

Comprehensive Sanitation & Protection Services

From initial audit to preventive maintenance, our specialists deploy targeted treatments using certified methods — compliant with European regulatory frameworks.

Insect Control

Targeted désinsectisation treatments for crawling and flying insects. Identification, treatment plan, and follow-up monitoring for long-term elimination.

Rodent Management

Professional dératisation for rats, mice, and other rodents. Integrated pest management combining baiting, exclusion, and environmental assessment.

Disinfection & Decontamination

Complete surface and air disinfection using CE-certified biocidal products. Suitable for food production facilities, healthcare, and hospitality environments.

Hygiene Audits

Independent HACCP-aligned hygiene audits with written reports. Identify risks, close compliance gaps, and meet BRC, IFS, or ISO 22000 standards.

Fumigation Services

Controlled fumigation for warehouses, food storage, and transport vehicles. Full documentation supplied for regulatory records and audit trails.

Preventive Treatments

Scheduled preventive programmes tailored to your facility risk profile — reducing the probability of infestation before it disrupts your operations.
