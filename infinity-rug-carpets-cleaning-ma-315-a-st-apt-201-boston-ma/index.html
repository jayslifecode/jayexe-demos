What We Do

Expert Cleaning for Every Surface

From delicate oriental heirlooms to wall-to-wall carpet, we handle every fabric with precision and care.

Oriental Rug Cleaning

Hand-washed using specialized techniques that protect delicate fibers, vibrant dyes, and intricate patterns in heirloom-quality rugs.

Carpet Deep Cleaning

Hot water extraction removes embedded dirt, allergens, and bacteria from wall-to-wall carpet, restoring softness and color.

Upholstery Cleaning

Safe, fabric-matched cleaning for sofas, chairs, and cushions. We refresh your furniture without shrinking, fading, or damaging it.

Stain & Odor Removal

Professional-grade treatments that lift stubborn stains and neutralize odors at the source — not just masking them.

Area Rug Cleaning

In-plant cleaning for all area rug types — wool, silk, synthetic, and blends. Full inspection, wash, rinse, and drying included.

Pet Hair & Allergen Removal

Deep extraction removes embedded pet hair, dander, and allergens, creating a cleaner and healthier environment for your family.
