About Kairos

SERVING CORAL GABLES WITH INTEGRITY

Kairos Pest Control was founded on a simple belief: every family and business deserves a safe, pest-free environment. Our certified technicians bring deep local knowledge and industry-leading techniques to every job.

We use Integrated Pest Management (IPM) strategies that minimize chemical use while maximizing results. Our treatments are safe for children, pets, and the South Florida ecosystem.
