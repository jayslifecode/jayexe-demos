What We Offer

OUR BLIND
COLLECTION

From sleek rollers to classic venetians — we stock and fit every style of blind to suit any room and any budget.

01

Roller Blinds

Clean, minimal and endlessly versatile. Available in hundreds of fabrics including blackout, dimout and moisture-resistant options for every room.

02

Venetian Blinds

Timeless aluminium or real wood slats. Adjust the angle to control exactly how much light enters — perfect for offices and living spaces alike.

03

Roman Blinds

Soft fabric folds that add warmth and texture to any room. Choose from our extensive range of plains, patterns and blackout linings.

04

Vertical Blinds

Ideal for large windows and patio doors. Rotate the louvres for precise light control or draw them fully open for an unobstructed view.

05

Blackout Blinds

Complete light exclusion for bedrooms, nurseries and home cinemas. Available in roller, roman and venetian styles to suit your décor.

06

Commercial Blinds

Offices, schools and hospitality across Edinburgh. Durable, stylish blinds supplied and fitted with minimal disruption to your operations.
