What We Offer

Crafted for Every Window, Every Room

Roller Blinds

Clean, architectural lines in an extensive fabric library — blackout, sheer, or solar. The purist's window treatment, tailored to the millimetre.

Plantation Shutters

Timeless solid louvre shutters, crafted in hardwood or MDF to your exact frame. Precision-engineered tilt rods, a permanent architectural statement.

Roman Blinds

Soft, structured folds that bring warmth and texture. Choose from our curated luxury fabric collection — interlined for superior light control and insulation.

Venetian Blinds

Aluminium or real wood slats in every width and finish. Adjust light by the degree — the original precision light filter, reinvented for modern interiors.

Perfect Fit Blinds

Frame-mounted with no visible brackets or cords — a seamless, child-safe solution that clips directly into your window beading for a factory-fitted finish.

Bespoke Made-to-Measure

Arched windows, bay windows, conservatories, Velux — no shape is too demanding. Every measurement taken on-site, every product crafted to your exact specification.
