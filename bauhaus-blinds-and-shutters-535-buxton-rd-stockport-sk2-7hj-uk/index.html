What we offer

MADE-TO-MEASURE BLINDS & SHUTTERS

Every window is different. We measure, advise, and fit all styles of blinds and shutters to your exact specifications — with a finish that lasts.

01

Roller Blinds

Clean, modern roller blinds in blackout, day & night, and printed fabrics. Made to your exact window width and drop, fitted perfectly every time.

02

Venetian Blinds

Aluminium and real wood venetian blinds for precise light control. Timeless style for living rooms, kitchens, and home offices throughout Stockport.

03

Plantation Shutters

Premium hardwood and MDF plantation shutters, custom built for each room. Full-height, tier-on-tier, and café-style configurations available.

04

Roman Blinds

Elegant fabric roman blinds in hundreds of patterns and textures. Adds warmth and character to bedrooms, living rooms, and bay windows.

05

Vertical Blinds

Practical vertical blinds ideal for large windows, patio doors, and commercial spaces. Available in fabric, PVC, and blackout options.

06

Made-to-Measure Fitting

We measure every window ourselves and handle the complete installation. No flat-pack, no guesswork — just a professional finish guaranteed.
