What We Do

Specialist Services for Every Situation

From a single residential treatment to ongoing commercial monitoring — we tailor every intervention to your specific environment and needs.

Rodent Control

Comprehensive dératisation for homes and businesses. Targeted baiting, trapping, and entry-point sealing to eliminate and prevent reinfestation.

Insect Treatment

Professional désinsectisation for all crawling and flying insects — ants, cockroaches, bedbugs, wasps, and more. Targeted, minimal-residue methods.

Disinfection & Odour Treatment

Full-spectrum disinfection and odour neutralisation for indoor and outdoor spaces. Effective against bacteria, viruses, and organic odour sources.

Pigeon & Bird Control

Humane deterrent systems and netting to protect buildings and infrastructure from pigeon damage — without harm to the animals.

Wood Treatment

Curative and preventive treatment for wood-boring insects and dry rot. Protecting structural timber in residential and heritage properties.

Commercial & Preventive Monitoring

Scheduled inspection and monitoring contracts for restaurants, warehouses, offices, and food-processing environments. Full compliance documentation.

Our Approach

Our Commitment to Responsible Pest Management

Pest control does not have to mean chemical aggression. Our methods prioritise targeted intervention, minimal environmental impact, and lasting prevention over repeated treatment.

Low-Impact Treatments

We select the least invasive effective product for each situation — reducing chemical load on your environment.

Inspect Before You Treat

Every visit begins with a thorough inspection. We diagnose root causes, not just symptoms, to avoid unnecessary applications.

Prevention-First Philosophy

Structural advice, entry-point sealing, and habitat modification to prevent pests returning — reducing the need for repeated interventions.

Certified Professionals

All technicians operate in compliance with Luxembourg's hygiene and pesticide regulations, with full traceability of every intervention.
