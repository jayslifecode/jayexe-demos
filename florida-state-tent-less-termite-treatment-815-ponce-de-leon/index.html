The No-Tent Difference

Why Choose Tent-less Treatment?

Traditional fumigation disrupts your life for days. Our precision tent-less approach eliminates termites without uprooting your family — at a fraction of the cost.

The Old Way

Traditional Tent Fumigation

  • Vacate your home for 2–3 days minimum
  • Remove all food, medications, cosmetics, and plants
  • Risk of roof damage and insurance claims
  • Requires tree and shrub trimming around home
  • Disconnect all antennas, satellite dishes, security systems
  • High cost — and neighbors can see your home is tented
VS
The Better Way

Our Precision Tent-less Method

  • Stay in your home throughout the entire treatment
  • No removal of food, medications, cosmetics, or plants
  • Zero roof damage — eliminates insurance headaches
  • No landscaping prep required — no shrub or tree trimming
  • No disconnecting antennas, satellites, or security systems
  • Safe, affordable — a fraction of the cost of tenting
What We Do

Termite Services We Offer

Exclusively tent-less — every service, every time. We do not tent, and we do not perform other types of pest control. This is all we do, and we do it exceptionally well.

Tent-less Termite Treatment

Our flagship service. EPA-registered termiticides applied precisely to eliminate active infestations — without a tent, without disruption to your daily life.

Drywood Termite Control

Targeted treatment for drywood termite colonies that nest within the wood of your home. Precision wood treatments reach colonies directly without fumigation.

Subterranean Termite Control

Conventional liquid barrier and soil treatments that create a protective zone around your foundation, stopping subterranean colonies from entering.

Termite Inspection

State-licensed WDO inspectors conduct thorough inspections of your property. Required for real estate transactions and essential for early detection.

Wood Damage Repair

Beyond treatment, we help restore structural wood damage caused by termite activity. Comprehensive remediation to get your home back to full strength.

Prevention & Monitoring

Preventive treatments and free follow-up inspections keep termites from returning. Ongoing monitoring gives you year-round peace of mind.
