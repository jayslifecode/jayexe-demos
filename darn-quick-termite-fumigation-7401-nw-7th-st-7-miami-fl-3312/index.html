What We Do

Termite Services,
Done Right & Done Fast

Complete termite and pest solutions for Miami-area homes and businesses. Certified operators. Proven treatments.

Tent Fumigation

Full-structure Vikane gas fumigation — the most effective method for drywood termite infestations. 100% eradication, guaranteed.

Drywood Termite Treatment

Targeted treatments for drywood termite colonies in walls, attics, and structural wood — without the disruption of full fumigation.

Subterranean Termite Control

Liquid soil treatments and bait systems to eliminate underground colonies before they compromise your foundation.

Spot Treatment

Precision localized treatment for early-stage or isolated infestations — a faster, lower-cost alternative when the damage is contained.

Termite Inspection

Thorough same-day inspections by certified operators — identify the species, severity, and best course of action before it gets worse.

Prevention Program

Ongoing Integrated Pest Management to keep termites and pests away year-round. Customized for South Florida’s climate and conditions.
