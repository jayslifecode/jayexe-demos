What we do

COMPLETE TERMITE PROTECTION

From full tent fumigation to targeted no-tent treatments — proven methods for every termite problem Miami throws at you.

01

Termite Inspections

Thorough whole-property inspections identifying active colonies, damage areas, and entry points. Written report included at no cost.

02

Tent Fumigation

Full structural fumigation using Vikane gas — the gold standard for drywood termite elimination. Safe, documented, and highly effective.

03

No-Tent Treatment

Targeted termite treatment without the hassle of tenting. Ideal for localized infestations in condos, townhomes, and occupied spaces.

04

Drywood Termite Control

Specialized treatments for drywood species common throughout South Florida — the most destructive type found in Miami structures.

05

Subterranean Barriers

Liquid soil treatments and baiting systems creating continuous underground barriers against subterranean termite colonies.

06

Annual Protection Plans

Year-round monitoring, inspection visits, and priority service — protecting your property from re-infestation and future damage.
