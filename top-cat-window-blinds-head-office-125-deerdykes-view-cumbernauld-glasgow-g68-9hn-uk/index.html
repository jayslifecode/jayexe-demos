Our Products

Blinds & Shutters for Every Home

From classic roller blinds to bespoke plantation shutters — all crafted and fitted to the highest Scottish standard.

Roller Blinds

Sleek, versatile and easy to operate. Available in hundreds of fabrics including blockout, sunscreen and translucent options to suit any room.

Vertical Blinds

Perfect for large windows and patio doors. Smooth light control with a clean, contemporary aesthetic that works in any space.

Venetian Blinds

Timeless and precise. Aluminium or wooden slats that tilt to your exact preference, giving you complete control over light and privacy.

Plantation Shutters

The ultimate window dressing. Solid, beautiful and built to last — plantation shutters add permanent value and kerb appeal to any property.

Roman Blinds

Elegant fabric folds that bring warmth and texture. Custom made to your window measurements in a vast selection of premium fabrics.

Blackout Blinds

Total darkness when you need it. Ideal for bedrooms and media rooms — our blackout range blocks up to 99% of incoming light.
