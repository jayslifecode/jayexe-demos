What We Treat

Precision Pest Control for Every Threat

From stubborn cockroaches to hidden termite colonies, AL-FLEX deploys science-backed treatments that eliminate pests at every life stage.

Cockroach Control

Targeted baiting, flushing, and residual treatments that eliminate German, American, and Oriental roaches — and prevent reinfestation.

Rodent Extermination

Integrated rodent programs that identify entry points, deploy strategic trapping, and seal gaps so rats and mice stay out for good.

Ant Elimination

Colony-targeting baits and exterior barriers for fire ants, ghost ants, carpenter ants, and every other species native to South Florida.

Termite Treatment

Full-structure tenting with Vikane gas, Termidor liquid treatments, baiting systems, and pre-construction soil treatment — Florida's largest fumigator.

Bed Bug Control

Comprehensive heat and chemical treatment protocols designed for hotels, residential buildings, and single-family homes with minimal disruption.

Mosquito Treatment

Larvicide applications and barrier sprays that drastically reduce mosquito populations around your property throughout the season.
